Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We do not store your personal messages or contacts. When you use our session generator, we only process the technical authentication data required by WhatsApp to establish a secure connection. This data is stored locally on your device or temporarily in our secure environment during the session generation process.

2. How We Use Data

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of generating a WhatsApp Session ID. This ID allows you to connect your account to third-party applications or bots. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

3. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information. All session data is encrypted and handled using industry-standard protocols. Once the session is generated and sent to you, the temporary authentication files are immediately deleted from our servers.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact us at support@lavvorstudio.com.
